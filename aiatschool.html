AI@School: Artificial Intelligence for School Students

  • Workshop designed for school students to spread awareness on AI

  • A hands-on tutorial to learn about basic theories as welll as develop an AI driven system

  • Designed for students from class 8 to class 12

Content at high level

  1. Introduction to Machine Learning (ML) and Deep Learning (DL)

    1. the most powerful sub-area of AI

  2. Introduction to Computer Vision (CV) or Natural Language Processing (NLP)

    1. two most popular application domains

  3. Application on Object detection using ML and CV

    1. hands-on session using Colab and Tensorflow

  4. Building Image Classification tools using Machines https:teachablemachine.withgoogle.com/

Workshops conducted

  1. The first workshop was conducted for Kendriya Vidyalaya Kanjikode in Nov 2021

    1. Students developed object tracking software

  2. The second workshop was conducted as part of Science Quest and Crystal Program at IIT Palakkad in May 2022

    1. Students built an AI tool to classify images
