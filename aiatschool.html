AI@School: Artificial Intelligence for School Students

  • A workshop designed for school students to spread awareness on AI

  • A hands-on tutorial to learn about basic theories as welll as develop an AI driven system

Content at high level

  1. Introduction to Machine Learning (ML) and Deep Learning (DL)

    1. the most powerful sub-area of AI

  2. Introduction to Computer Vision (CV) or Natural Language Processing (NLP)

    1. two most popular application domains

  3. Application on Object detection using ML and CV

    1. hands-on session using Colab and Tensorflow

First workshop was conducted for Kendriya Vidyalaya Kanjikode in Nov 2021

  • Students developed object tracking software
